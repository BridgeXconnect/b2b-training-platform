# Business Case & ROI Analysis
## B2B English Training Platform

## Executive Summary

The B2B English Training Platform represents a strategic investment in the rapidly growing corporate language learning market, projected to reach $8.5 billion by 2027. This comprehensive business case demonstrates a compelling ROI of 312% over 3 years, with break-even achieved in Month 18.

### Key Financial Highlights
- **Total Investment**: $2.4M over 24 months
- **3-Year Revenue Projection**: $9.9M
- **Net Present Value (NPV)**: $4.2M
- **Internal Rate of Return (IRR)**: 89%
- **Payback Period**: 18 months
